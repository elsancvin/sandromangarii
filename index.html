Kabasaran Dance

The Kabasaran dance is a traditional dance from North Sulawesi, Indonesia. Usually performed in traditional ceremonies or cultural festivals. This dance involves dynamic movements depicting strength and courage, often accompanied by traditional music and singing. The Kabasaran dance is often a symbol of the strength and spirit of the Minahasa people.
